Chat R ules No Homophobia, Transphobia, Racism, or Ableism
Any act of t his will not be tolerated this includes the use of slurs and offensive language towards these groups!

No Spamming
It's annoying to deal with spamming and causes many problems for moderation so don't do it. This includes repetition of emotes and symbols try to keep the amount you use to under 15 otherwise it might trigger auto-mod.

No sexualising me
Literally don't sexualise me through the language you use or anything else that could seem like you are.

Don't put any personal info in chat
Putting any personal info in chat whether yours or someone elses is strictly forbidden it is extremely dangerous and you will be permanentlybanned if you try.

Be respectful
Treat everyone kindly and if you have anything to report then tell one of the moderators.

No Mini Modding
Don't try to moderate people yourself if you see someone break any of the rules just tell a moderator so they can take care of it.

Listen to moderation
The moderation team is here to keep the stream safe, managed, and running. They have final say when it comes to moderation so listen to them.

Avoid religious and or political discussions
Just don't have these kind of discussions unless stated otherwise in the stream.

Don't post links in chat
Links can be rather unsafe so don't send links unless it is stated otherwise in which case certain links will be allo wed but only certain types of links like youtube links will be allowed for that period.

No advertising or self-promotion
Streams are not your billboard so don't be trying to use them as such. Self promotion is prohibited unless strictly stated otherwise.

No game spoilers
If I'm playing a story based game don't post spoilers it ruins the game for me and everyone else in the stream.

Keep chat in English
Since I speak english please keep chat to English as otherwise I won't understand what is being said and it would also make it hard to moderate the stream since it's English based.
